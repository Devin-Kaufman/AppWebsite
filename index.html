Project Background

The Advanced Antenna Design team uses innovative methods to design, simulate, manufacture, and test antennas. We utilize resources such as the CSU antennas test range and B110 electromagnetics lab to create new antennas and RF devices.

Goals

Our team aims to develop new antenna design techniques and RF hardware while working to maintain and operate facilities such as the Advanced Antenna Design to test, measure, and improve our devices.
