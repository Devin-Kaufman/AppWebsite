Goals and Objectives

Primary Objectives:

  • Measure dielectric to reduce simulation error of algorithmically generated antennas
  • Manufacture and measure generative antenna to verify simulation results
  • Evaluate safety and effectiveness of ATR
  • Successfully operate ATR
  • Ensure all designs and operations comply with IEEE Std. C95.1 (IEEE electromagnetic safety standards)

Secondary Objectives:

  • 3D print an antenna mount for chamber testing of PCB antenna
  • Design a multi-layer antenna
  • Design antennas for radar applications
  • Design and manufacture new matching circuits for antennas
